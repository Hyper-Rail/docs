## Overview

HyperRail supports two primary account types:

- **Merchants** — businesses that perform on-ramp, off-ramp, and fiat exchange transactions  
- **Exchange Client** — institutions that supply fiat or stablecoin liquidity to power transactions on the platform  

Choose the role that best matches how you intend to use HyperRail.
