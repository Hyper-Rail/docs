## Getting Started & Account Access

<Accordion title="Q1: What is HyperRail used for?">
A: HyperRail enables businesses to convert between fiat currencies and stablecoins, perform fiat-to-fiat exchange, and manage liquidity using stablecoins as the settlement rail.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q2: Is HyperRail an API or a dashboard application?">
A: HyperRail is currently a **dashboard-based application**. All transactions, treasury operations, and liquidity management are performed via the platform UI.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q3: Who can use HyperRail?">
A: HyperRail is built for:
- Merchants performing on-ramp, off-ramp, and FX transactions  
- Exchange Client supplying stablecoin or fiat liquidity  
- Enterprises managing cross-border treasury operations
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q4: How do I create a HyperRail account?">
A: Sign up directly on the HyperRail application and complete onboarding based on your role (Merchant or Liquidity Provider).
</Accordion>

## On-Ramp Transactions

<Accordion title="Q5: What is an on-ramp transaction?">
A: An on-ramp converts **fiat currency into stablecoins**, crediting the stablecoins to your HyperRail wallet.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q6: What fiat currencies and stablecoins are supported?">
A: HyperRail supports NGN and GHS for local fiat currencies for now  and major stablecoins like USDT, USDC and Matic.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q7: How long does an on-ramp transaction take?">
A: Settlement times depend on the fiat corridor, but most on-ramps complete within minutes to a few business hours.
</Accordion>

---

## Off-Ramp Transactions

<Accordion title="Q8: What is an off-ramp transaction?">
A: An off-ramp converts **stablecoins into fiat currency**, which is then paid out to a local bank account.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q9: Where can off-ramp funds be sent?">
A: Funds can be sent to supported local bank accounts linked to your HyperRail account.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q10: Are off-ramp rates fixed?">
A: Rates are executable at the time of transaction and reflect current liquidity and market conditions.
</Accordion>

## Fiat Currency Exchange

<Accordion title="Q11: How does fiat-to-fiat exchange work on HyperRail?">
A: Fiat-to-fiat exchange is executed via stablecoins:  
Fiat A → Stablecoin → Fiat B.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q12: Why does HyperRail use stablecoins for FX?">
A: Stablecoins enable faster settlement, lower costs, and better transparency compared to traditional correspondent banking.
</Accordion>

## Liquidity & Pricing

<Accordion title="Q13: Where does HyperRail’s liquidity come from?">
A: Liquidity is aggregated from multiple exchange client across fiat corridors and stablecoin markets.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q14: Who can become a liquidity provider on HyperRail?">
A: Approved institutions and entities with fiat or stablecoin liquidity can onboard as Exchange Client.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q15: How are exchange rates determined?">
A: Rates are calculated using aggregated liquidity and market pricing, with spreads dynamically adjusted based on transaction volume and liquidity depth.
</Accordion>

## Wallets & Treasury

<Accordion title="Q16: Does HyperRail provide stablecoin wallets?">
A: Yes. HyperRail provides merchant-owned stablecoin wallets with simplified key management.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q17: Can I hold both fiat and stablecoins on HyperRail?">
A: Yes. You can view and manage fiat balances, stablecoin balances, and in-flight transactions from a unified dashboard.
</Accordion>

## Security & Compliance

<Accordion title="Q18: Is HyperRail secure?">
A: HyperRail is built with enterprise-grade security, including wallet safeguards, role-based access, and transaction monitoring.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q19: Does HyperRail require KYC/KYB?">
A: Yes. Merchants and exchange client must complete onboarding and verification before transacting.
</Accordion>

## Team & Access Control

<Accordion title="Q20: Can I add team members to my HyperRail account?">
A: Yes. You can invite team members and assign roles with specific permissions.
</Accordion>

<Accordion title="Q21: Can different team members have different access levels?">
A: Yes. Roles allow you to control who can view balances, initiate transactions, or manage settings.
</Accordion>
