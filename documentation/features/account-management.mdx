## Overview

Account Management allows you to **view and update your personal and business information** on HyperRail.  
This helps keep your account accurate, secure, and tailored to your organization.

---

## My Profile: Personalize Your Account

You can customize your account by managing your **contact details** and **brand identity**, ensuring a consistent experience across the platform.
